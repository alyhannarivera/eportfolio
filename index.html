De La Salle University. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - Software Technology.

After getting a scholarship, I started with my journey. On the first year, I was exposed to the different subfields of Computer Science. At first, I got confused on which specialization I should take but I was then enlightened which one I am enjoying. I took Software technology and accomplished a lot of projects. Most of these projects are group projects.

There are cases where I am the follower. On the contrary, being a leader in a group is inevitable. On my previous projects, what always happens is that I will give each member a task. If a member did not report to me for so long, I will do his/her part and, will end up doing everything. I am letting them be a free-loader with the belief that it is their loss since they would not learn anything. However, I have realized that I am being selfish. I should push them to do their part so they could learn something.

Sometimes, I am on the front end. Sometimes, I am on the back end. I have experiences in User Experience, User Interface, Database, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Startup Engineering.

Our thesis is a picture-based automatic story generator for children. It is known as Picture Books 4 and is under the field of Natural Language Processing. Its significance to computing is that it can be used for further research about Story generation while its significance to society is that it can teach the children how to interact properly with other people.

Knowing that I am a person who always wants to take on challenges, I applied for it and got in. The program started last third year-second term. There is only one course per term. This program did not cease to challenge me. Since we have been taking up our Masters degree for two terms, we have a few accomplishments as of yet. On the contrary, I have already learned a lot from these projects.
